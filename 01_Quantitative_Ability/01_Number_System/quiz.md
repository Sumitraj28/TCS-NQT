# Number System — Quiz

## Rapid-Fire Q&A
1. **What is the divisibility rule for 11?**
   - **Answer:** The absolute difference between the sum of digits at odd positions and even positions is 0 or a multiple of 11.
2. **What is the number of factors of 100?**
   - **Answer:** $100 = 2^2 \times 5^2 \rightarrow (2+1)(2+1) = 9$ factors.
3. **What is the cyclicity cycle length of digit 2?**
   - **Answer:** 4 ($2, 4, 8, 6 \dots$).
4. **How do you find the LCM of fractions?**
   - **Answer:** $\frac{\text{LCM of Numerators}}{\text{HCF of Denominators}}$.
5. **What is the number of trailing zeros in $25!$?**
   - **Answer:** $\lfloor 25/5 \rfloor + \lfloor 25/25 \rfloor = 5 + 1 = 6$ zeros.
6. **Are 8 and 15 coprime?**
   - **Answer:** Yes ($\text{HCF}(8, 15) = 1$).
7. **What is the product of LCM and HCF of two numbers $A$ and $B$?**
   - **Answer:** $A \times B$.
8. **What is the hexadecimal value of binary `1111`?**
   - **Answer:** `F`.
9. **How many prime numbers exist between 1 and 50?**
   - **Answer:** 15.
10. **What is the remainder when $18^{10}$ is divided by 17?**
    - **Answer:** $18 \equiv 1 \pmod{17} \rightarrow 1^{10} \equiv 1 \pmod{17}$. Remainder is **1**.

## True or False
1. **Every perfect square has an even number of factors.**
   - **Answer:** False (they always have an odd number of factors).
2. **The number 1 is a prime number.**
   - **Answer:** False.
3. **If a number is divisible by 3 and 4, it is also divisible by 12.**
   - **Answer:** True.
4. **The binary number `101` is equivalent to decimal 5.**
   - **Answer:** True.
5. **A negative number remainder can be simplified by adding the divisor.**
   - **Answer:** True.
