# Number System — Practice: Basic (15 Questions)

---

### Q1 — Odd/Even Check
What is the condition for a number to be even?
**Answer:** The unit digit is divisible by 2 (ends in 0, 2, 4, 6, 8).

---

### Q2 — Divisibility by 3
Is 456 divisible by 3?
**Answer:** Yes. Sum of digits is $4+5+6 = 15$, which is divisible by 3.

---

### Q3 — Divisibility by 5
What digits can a number end in to be divisible by 5?
**Answer:** 0 or 5.

---

### Q4 — Prime Factorization of 24
**Answer:** $24 = 2^3 \times 3^1$.

---

### Q5 — HCF of 15 and 25
**Answer:** 5.

---

### Q6 — LCM of 8 and 12
**Answer:** 24.

---

### Q7 — Number of Factors of 12
**Answer:** $12 = 2^2 \times 3^1 \rightarrow (2+1)(1+1) = 6$ factors. (Factors: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12).

---

### Q8 — Trailing Zeros in $10!$
**Answer:** $\lfloor 10/5 \rfloor = 2$ zeros.

---

### Q9 — Convert Binary `1010` to Decimal
**Answer:** $1 \times 2^3 + 0 \times 2^2 + 1 \times 2^1 + 0 \times 2^0 = 8 + 0 + 2 + 0 = 10$.

---

### Q10 — Last Digit of $5^{23}$
**Answer:** 5 (any power of 5 ends in 5).

---

### Q11 — Base 10 representation of Octal `12`
**Answer:** $1 \times 8^1 + 2 \times 8^0 = 8 + 2 = 10$.

---

### Q12 — Least Common Multiple of coprime numbers
What is the LCM of two coprime numbers $A$ and $B$?
**Answer:** $A \times B$.

---

### Q13 — Remainder of $14 \bmod 3$
**Answer:** 2.

---

### Q14 — Is 93 prime?
**Answer:** No. $9+3=12$, which is divisible by 3 (factors: 1, 3, 31, 93).

---

### Q15 — Convert Decimal 15 to Binary
**Answer:** $15 = 1111_2$.
