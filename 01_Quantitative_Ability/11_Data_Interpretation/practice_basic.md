# Data Interpretation — Practice: Basic

---

## Dataset — Demographic Profile of Five Cities

The table below shows the male and female population (in thousands) and the overall literacy rate (%) of five cities.

| City | Male Population (thousands) | Female Population (thousands) | Literacy Rate (%) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| City A | $120$ | $100$ | $80\%$ |
| City B | $150$ | $130$ | $70\%$ |
| City C | $90$ | $80$ | $85\%$ |
| City D | $200$ | $180$ | $75\%$ |
| City E | $80$ | $70$ | $90\%$ |

---

### Q1 — Total Female Population
What is the total female population across all five cities combined?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:** Sum the female populations of all cities:
    $$\text{Total} = 100 + 130 + 80 + 180 + 70 = 560\text{ thousand}$$
*   **Answer:** $560,000$ (or $560\text{ thousand}$)

---

### Q2 — Sex Ratio (M:F) of City C
What is the ratio of male to female population in City C?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  $\text{Male Population of C} = 90$.
    2.  $\text{Female Population of C} = 80$.
    3.  Ratio: $\frac{90}{80} = 9 : 8$.
*   **Answer:** $9 : 8$

---

### Q3 — Average Male Population
What is the average male population across the five cities?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  Sum the male populations: $120 + 150 + 90 + 200 + 80 = 640\text{ thousand}$.
    2.  Divide by the number of cities ($5$): $\frac{640}{5} = 128\text{ thousand}$.
*   **Answer:** $128,000$ (or $128\text{ thousand}$)

---

### Q4 — Literate Population of City E
How many literate people are there in City E?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  $\text{Total Population of City E} = \text{Male} + \text{Female} = 80 + 70 = 150\text{ thousand}$.
    2.  Literacy rate of City E = $90\%$.
    3.  $\text{Literate population} = 90\% \text{ of } 150 = \frac{90}{100} \times 150 = 135\text{ thousand}$.
*   **Answer:** $135,000$ (or $135\text{ thousand}$)

---

### Q5 — Highest Female Population
Which city has the highest female population?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:** Looking at the female population column:
    *   City A: 100, City B: 130, City C: 80, City D: 180, City E: 70.
    *   180 (City D) is the largest value.
*   **Answer:** City D
