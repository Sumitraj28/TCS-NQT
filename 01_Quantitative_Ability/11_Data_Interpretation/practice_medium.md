# Data Interpretation — Practice: Medium

---

## Dataset — Corporate Quarterly Expenditures

The following table details the quarterly expenditures (in thousand USD) of a firm across different expense categories.

| Expense Head | Q1 ($k) | Q2 ($k) | Q3 ($k) | Q4 ($k) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Salaries | $500$ | $520$ | $550$ | $580$ |
| Infrastructure | $150$ | $180$ | $160$ | $200$ |
| R&D | $80$ | $90$ | $110$ | $120$ |
| Marketing | $120$ | $150$ | $140$ | $180$ |

---

### Q1 — Salary Growth
By what percentage did Salaries expenditure increase from Q1 to Q4?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  $\text{Salaries}_{Q1} = 500$, $\text{Salaries}_{Q4} = 580$.
    2.  $\text{Percentage Increase} = \frac{580 - 500}{500} \times 100\% = \frac{80}{500} \times 100\% = 16\%$.
*   **Answer:** $16\%$

---

### Q2 — Expenditure Ratios
What is the ratio of combined R&D and Marketing expenditure in Q2 to the combined Salaries and Infrastructure expenditure in Q3?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  $\text{Combined R\&D and Marketing in Q2} = 90 + 150 = 240$.
    2.  $\text{Combined Salaries and Infrastructure in Q3} = 550 + 160 = 710$.
    3.  Ratio: $\frac{240}{710} = 24 : 71$.
*   **Answer:** $24 : 71$

---

### Q3 — Average Infrastructure Spend
What is the average expenditure per quarter on Infrastructure?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  Sum the Infrastructure expenditures: $150 + 180 + 160 + 200 = 690$.
    2.  Divide by 4 quarters: $\frac{690}{4} = 172.5$.
*   **Answer:** $\$172,500$ (or $172.5\text{ thousand USD}$)

---

### Q4 — Share of Marketing in Q1
What percentage of the total Q1 expenditure was spent on Marketing?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  Calculate total expenditure in Q1:
        $$\text{Total}_{Q1} = 500 + 150 + 80 + 120 = 850$$.
    2.  Marketing in Q1 = 120.
    3.  $\text{Percentage Share} = \frac{120}{850} \times 100\% = \frac{12}{85} \times 100\% \approx 14.12\%$.
*   **Answer:** $14.12\%$

---

### Q5 — R&D Share of Budget
In which quarter was the percentage of R&D expenditure to the total quarterly expenditure the highest?
*   **Step-by-step Solution:**
    1.  Calculate total expenditures for each quarter:
        *   Q1: $500 + 150 + 80 + 120 = 850$
        *   Q2: $520 + 180 + 90 + 150 = 940$
        *   Q3: $550 + 160 + 110 + 140 = 960$
        *   Q4: $580 + 200 + 120 + 180 = 1080$
    2.  Calculate R&D percentage for each quarter:
        *   Q1: $\frac{80}{850} \approx 9.41\%$
        *   Q2: $\frac{90}{940} \approx 9.57\%$
        *   Q3: $\frac{110}{960} \approx 11.46\%$
        *   Q4: $\frac{120}{1080} \approx 11.11\%$
    3.  Comparing values, Q3 has the highest percentage ($11.46\%$).
*   **Answer:** Q3
