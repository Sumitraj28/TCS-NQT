# Email & Paragraph Writing — Practice: Advanced

---

## Part 1 — Email Writing Prompts

Write a professional email for each of the following scenarios. Each response must be between 80–120 words.

### Prompt 1 — Service Outage Apology (B2B)
*   **Scenario:** Your software startup experienced a 3-hour server outage. Email a major client representative apologizing for the downtime.
*   **Guidelines:** Acknowledge the issue, explain the resolution steps taken to prevent recurrence, and assure them of future stability.

### Prompt 2 — Negotiating Payment Terms
*   **Scenario:** A vendor proposes a 100% upfront payment for a software module. Email them requesting a milestone-based plan (e.g., 40/60).
*   **Guidelines:** State corporate finance policies as the reason, propose the new structure, and keep the tone highly collaborative.

### Prompt 3 — Recommending Tool Upgrade
*   **Scenario:** Persuade your division director to upgrade the team's project management tool from a free version to a paid tier.
*   **Guidelines:** Contrast the features, estimate time savings, and provide a cost-benefit statement.

## Part 2 — Paragraph Writing Prompts

Write a unified paragraph for each of the following topics. Each response must be between 120–150 words.

### Prompt 4 — The Future of Space Exploration
*   **Structure:** Intro $\rightarrow$ Private sector participation and Martian colonisation dreams $\rightarrow$ Example (SpaceX or Artemis program) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Discuss scientific curiosity vs. economic constraints.

### Prompt 5 — Sustainable Agriculture
*   **Structure:** Intro $\rightarrow$ Soil health conservation and organic fertilizers $\rightarrow$ Example (crop rotation benefits) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Address food security challenges in a warming world.
