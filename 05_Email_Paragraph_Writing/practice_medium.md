# Email & Paragraph Writing — Practice: Medium

---

## Part 1 — Email Writing Prompts

Write a professional email for each of the following scenarios. Each response must be between 80–120 words.

### Prompt 1 — Budget Approval Request
*   **Scenario:** You need approval for a $500 budget to purchase an online research database subscription. Email your department head.
*   **Guidelines:** State the purpose, explain how it improves work efficiency, and detail the cost.

### Prompt 2 — Requesting Late Project Inputs
*   **Scenario:** A client has not sent the design assets needed for coding. Email them.
*   **Guidelines:** Explain the project timeline risk, state exactly what assets are missing, and request them by a specific date.

### Prompt 3 — Project Status Weekly Update
*   **Scenario:** Email your manager updating them on the weekly milestones completed by your team.
*   **Guidelines:** Use bullet points for accomplishments and mention next week's focus.

## Part 2 — Paragraph Writing Prompts

Write a unified paragraph for each of the following topics. Each response must be between 120–150 words.

### Prompt 4 — The Impact of Single-Use Plastics
*   **Structure:** Intro $\rightarrow$ Ecological damage $\rightarrow$ Example (ocean life stats) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Focus on environmental longevity of plastics.

### Prompt 5 — Challenges of Online Education
*   **Structure:** Intro $\rightarrow$ Lack of physical interaction/attention issues $\rightarrow$ Example (distraction during live classes) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Balance the convenience of online learning with its psychological barriers.
