## ⚡ Shortcut 1: The Elimination Rule
*   **Shortcut:** Look for boundary properties (e.g. odd/even, positive/negative, units digit) to instantly eliminate incorrect choices.
*   **Real Number Demo:** If the output must be a multiple of 5, eliminate all options that do not end in 0 or 5.
