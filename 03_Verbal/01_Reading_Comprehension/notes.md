# Reading Comprehension — Complete Notes

---

## 1. Core RC Reading Strategies

To maximize accuracy and speed in the TCS NQT, avoid reading the passage like a novel. Use targeted reading techniques:

### A. Skimming
*   **What it is:** Read the passage rapidly (in 30-45 seconds) to get a high-level overview.
*   **How to do it:** Focus on the title, the first paragraph, the first sentence of each middle paragraph, and the final paragraph.
*   **Purpose:** Understand the **tone**, **topic**, and **structure** of the passage.

### B. Scanning
*   **What it is:** Looking for specific keywords or details without reading the surrounding sentences.
*   **How to do it:** Let your eyes sweep across the text looking for capitalized words (names, locations), numbers, or technical terms mentioned in the question.
*   **Purpose:** Locate detail-oriented facts quickly.

---

## 2. Tackling Specific Question Types

### A. Main-Idea Questions
*   **Prompt style:** "What is the primary purpose of the passage?" or "Which of the following is the most suitable title?"
*   **Strategy:** Look at the introduction and conclusion. Eliminate options that focus on a single detail from one paragraph rather than the passage as a whole.

### B. Inference Questions
*   **Prompt style:** "Which of the following can be inferred from the passage?" or "The author implies that..."
*   **Strategy:** An inference is something that is **not directly stated** but must be $100\%$ true based on the facts provided. Do not select options that require wild leaps of logic or are merely plausible but unproven.

### C. Vocabulary-in-Context Questions
*   **Prompt style:** "The word 'X' as used in paragraph 2 most nearly means..."
*   **Strategy:** Do not rely on your general definition of the word. Go back to the sentence, replace the word with a blank, and guess what word fits based on the surrounding context clues.
