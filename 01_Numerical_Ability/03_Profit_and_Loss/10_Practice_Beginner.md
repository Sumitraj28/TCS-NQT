# Profit & Loss Beginner Practice

This set contains 20 beginner-level questions to build formula familiarity.

**Q1.** An item is bought for ₹100 and sold for ₹125. Find the profit percentage.
> 🎯 Hint: Subtract CP from SP, then divide by CP.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
**Approach:** $\text{Profit}\% = \frac{SP - CP}{CP} \times 100$.  
**Step-by-step:**  
1. Profit $= 125 - 100 = 25$.  
2. $\text{Profit}\% = \frac{25}{100} \times 100 = 25\%$.  
**Answer:** **25**  
**Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 15 seconds
</details>
