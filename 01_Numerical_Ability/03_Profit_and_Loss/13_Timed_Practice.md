# Profit & Loss Timed Practice

### ⏱ Instructions
*   **Time Budget**: 12 minutes total (approx. 72 seconds per question).
*   **Format**: Non-MCQ style. Solve on paper and check the Answer Key at the bottom.

## 📝 Mock Test Questions

**Q1.** An item bought for ₹300 is sold for ₹360. Find the profit percentage.

**Q2.** Find the equivalent single discount for successive discounts of $15\%$ and $5\%$.

**Q3.** If the Cost Price of 24 articles equals the Selling Price of 18 articles, find the profit percentage.

**Q4.** A smart TV marked at ₹12,000 is sold at a discount of $15\%$. Find the Selling Price.

**Q5.** A merchant sells goods at Cost Price but uses a faulty weight of 940 grams instead of a kilogram. Find his profit percentage (round to 2 decimals).

**Q6.** A dealer marks his products up by $30\%$ and offers a $20\%$ discount. Find his net profit percentage.

**Q7.** Two soundbars are sold for ₹9,600 each. The seller makes a profit of $20\%$ on the first and a loss of $20\%$ on the second. Find the net loss percentage.

**Q8.** A shopkeeper advertises "Buy 4, get 2 free". What is the equivalent discount percentage?

**Q9.** A sells a phone to B at a $10\%$ profit. B sells it to C at a $10\%$ loss. If C pays ₹990, find A's Cost Price.

**Q10.** By selling a cycle for ₹540, a dealer loses $10\%$. What should be the Selling Price to make a profit of $10\%$?

### Solution to Q1:
*   $\text{Profit} = 360 - 300 = 60$.  
*   $\text{Profit}\% = \frac{60}{300} \times 100 = 20\%$.  
**Answer: 20**

---

### Solution to Q2:
*   $\text{Discount} = 15 + 5 - \frac{15 \times 5}{100} = 20 - 0.75 = 19.25\%$.  
**Answer: 19.25**

---

### Solution to Q3:
*   $\frac{SP}{CP} = \frac{24}{18} = \frac{4}{3} \implies \text{Profit}\% = \frac{1}{3} \times 100 = 33.33\%$.  
**Answer: 33.33**

---

### Solution to Q4:
*   $SP = 12000 \times 0.85 = 10,200$.  
**Answer: 10200**

---

### Solution to Q5:
*   $\text{Profit}\% = \frac{60}{940} \times 100 = \frac{3}{47} \times 100 \approx 6.38\%$.  
**Answer: 6.38**

---

### Solution to Q6:
*   $\text{Net} = 30 - 20 - \frac{30 \times 20}{100} = 10 - 6 = 4\%$.  
**Answer: 4**

---

### Solution to Q7:
*   $\text{Net Loss}\% = \left(\frac{20}{10}\right)^2\% = 4\%$.  
**Answer: 4**

---

### Solution to Q8:
*   $\text{Discount}\% = \frac{2}{4+2} \times 100 = \frac{1}{3} \times 100 = 33.33\%$.  
**Answer: 33.33**

---

### Solution to Q9:
*   $CP_A \times 1.10 \times 0.90 = 990 \implies CP_A \times 0.99 = 990 \implies CP_A = 1000$.  
**Answer: 1000**

---

### Solution to Q10:
*   $CP = \frac{540}{0.90} = 600$.  
*   $\text{New SP} = 600 \times 1.10 = 660$.  
**Answer: 660**
