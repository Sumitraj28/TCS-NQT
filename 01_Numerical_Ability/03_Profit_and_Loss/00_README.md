# 03 Profit & Loss Directory Overview

Welcome to the **Profit & Loss** module of the TCS NQT study guide. This folder contains all concepts, formulas, shortcuts, solved examples, and practice sets required to master commercial arithmetic for the exam.

---

## 📊 Module Weightage & Exam Relevance
*   **Section**: Section 1A (Numerical Ability)
*   **Average Questions**: 2 Questions per exam
*   **Target Time**: Under 75 seconds per question
*   **Prerequisites**: [02_Percentage](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/02_Percentage) (especially fraction conversion scales and multiplying factors).

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives
After completing this module, you will be able to:
1.  Instantly calculate Profit/Loss percentages and markup ratios.
2.  Solve complex successive markup and discount transactions in under 45 seconds.
3.  Tackle dishonest dealer and faulty weight tricks without formula confusion.
4.  Solve "Buy X, Get Y Free" promotional discounts mentally.
5.  Apply Selling Price equations to solve combined transactions.
