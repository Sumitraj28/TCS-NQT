# Direction Sense — Complete Notes

---

## 1. The Cardinal Directions

To solve direction puzzles without getting confused, always map directions to a standard 2D coordinate system:

*   **North (N):** Upwards ($+Y$ axis)
*   **South (S):** Downwards ($-Y$ axis)
*   **East (E):** Rightwards ($+X$ axis)
*   **West (W):** Leftwards ($-X$ axis)

### Cardinal vs. Intercardinal Directions
There are four intermediate directions located exactly midway ($45^\circ$) between the cardinal directions:
1.  **North-East (NE):** Between North and East.
2.  **North-West (NW):** Between North and West.
3.  **South-East (SE):** Between South and East.
4.  **South-West (SW):** Between South and West.

## 2. Left and Right Turn Logic

A common trap is turning left or right relative to your current heading rather than the page. Use this reference:

| Current Heading | A Turn to the **Right** results in... | A Turn to the **Left** results in... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Facing North** | East (90° clockwise) | West (90° counter-clockwise) |
| **Facing South** | West (90° clockwise) | East (90° counter-clockwise) |
| **Facing East** | South (90° clockwise) | North (90° counter-clockwise) |
| **Facing West** | North (90° clockwise) | South (90° counter-clockwise) |

*   **Rule of Thumb:**
    *   **Right Turn** always means turning **Clockwise**.
    *   **Left Turn** always means turning **Counter-Clockwise**.

## 3. Distance Calculations (Pythagoras Theorem)

To find the shortest distance (straight-line distance) between the starting point and finishing point, look for a right-angled triangle.

$$\text{Shortest Distance } (H) = \sqrt{\text{Base}^2 (B) + \text{Perpendicular}^2 (P)}$$

### Worked Example
A person walks $3\text{ km}$ North, turns East and walks $4\text{ km}$. How far is he from the starting point?
1.  The path forms a right-angled triangle with base $= 4\text{ km}$ and height $= 3\text{ km}$.
2.  $\text{Shortest Distance} = \sqrt{3^2 + 4^2} = \sqrt{9 + 16} = \sqrt{25} = 5\text{ km}$.
3.  The direction relative to the start point is North-East.
